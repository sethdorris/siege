If you're looking to join the group you should meet the following requirements:

  • Must have a Microphone
  • Must be active
  • Available to play at least once a week with the group
  • Can participate in the forums, even if one post per week
  • Must be mature
  • Can take a joke

Though not a requirement, it will help your chances on being accepted if you have at least a 1.0 K/D

After you apply, you will be contacted by email and asked to play with us over the course of a week (though a decision concerning your admittance to the group could be made before then) in order to make a decision on your application. We will evaluate the following.

  • how well you fit in with our group
  • how well you can communicate and relay information in game
  • and how active you are, both in game and in our forums.
